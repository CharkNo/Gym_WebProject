Accessibility @ ALL-AMERICAN

Here at ALL-AMERICAN FITNESS we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of accessibility options of different workout machines for everyone, no matter their condition.

We offer for our guests:

  • Cardio machines that don't require walking for those who struggle to walk

  • Open concept space in facilities so wheelchair bound members can still move to any area of the gym hassle free

  • Personal Trtainers to help our members reach their goals through their fitness journey

  • Staff members to assist with any questions, concerns, or to be a 'spotter' for a member during their lift
